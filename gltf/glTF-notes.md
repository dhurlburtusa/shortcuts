# glTF Notes

glTF™ is known as "The 3D Asset Delivery Format".

glTF™ is a royalty-free specification for the efficient transmission and loading of 3D scenes and models by engines and applications.

In 2022 glTF 2.0 was released as the ISO/IEC 12113:2022 International standard, recognizing glTF's global position as a 3D asset format.

See https://www.khronos.org/gltf/ for details.
